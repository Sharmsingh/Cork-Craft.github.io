Why You'll Love Us

Unlock the power of team building with Cork and Craft, where founders' expertise, science-driven approaches, and exclusive educational content merge to elevate your team's performance!

The Founders

Vashish Bansee UNH MAIOP '24 Cameryn Dick UNH MAIOP '24 Aima Shahid UNH MAIOP '24 Sharmilli Singh UNH MAIOP '24

Founded by a dynamic quartet of I/O Psychology graduate students from the University of New Haven, Cork and Craft is rooted in Connecticut's cultural landscape. Each founder brings a unique blend of passion, expertise, and innovative thinking to the craft beverage scene, leveraging their specialized knowledge to enhance team dynamics and performance through curated tasting experiences. Together, we are committed to transforming the way teams connect and collaborate, applying scientific principles to create truly impactful team-building events. It's our mission to blend the art of craft beverages with the science of team psychology, crafting experiences that leave lasting impressions.

The Science Behind Our Approach

Cork and Craft's tastings are designed to offer a unique blend of psychological and organizational benefits, grounded in the principles of Workplace Psychology, to foster a more cohesive, communicative, and high-performing team environment. Here's how our tastings contribute to these outcomes:

Foster Team Unity

Bridges gaps between team members, creating a sense of belonging and mutual support.

Enhances Communication

Encourages open dialogue and exchange of ideas, improving listening and speaking skills.

Boosts Performance

Stronger team bonds and communication lead to increased efficiency and creativity.

Reduces Stress

Engaging in a relaxed, enjoyable activity helps lower stress levels and prevent burnout.

Sparks Creativity

The unique tasting experience inspires creative thinking, enhancing team innovation.

Builds Cultural Awareness

Offers an opportunity for teams to explore and appreciate the diversity within local craft beverages, enriching team dynamics.

WorkLife with Adam Grant...

is a thought-provoking podcast that delves into the unique worlds of extraordinary workplaces, offering listeners insights into how to make work more enjoyable and rewarding. Hosted by organizational psychologist Adam Grant, the show explores topics ranging from the science of motivation to the art of collaboration, featuring stories and research that illuminate the path to a better work life.

The Journal of Applied Psychology...

publishes cutting-edge research on the application of psychological principles to understanding and solving problems encountered in the workplace and other organizational settings. It offers valuable insights into employee behavior, leadership dynamics, and organizational effectiveness.

The Connecticut Brewers Guild...

is an organization dedicated to promoting and advocating for the craft beer industry within the state of Connecticut. It serves as a comprehensive resource for beer enthusiasts and professionals alike, offering information on local breweries, craft beer events, and initiatives that support the growth and visibility of Connecticut's vibrant craft beer community.
