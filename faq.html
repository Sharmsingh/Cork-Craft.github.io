Frequently Asked Questions

Cork & Craft enriches team bonding experiences with curated local tastes, fostering connection and supporting community sustainability.

Cork and Craft proudly supports local Connecticut wineries and breweries by exclusively partnering with these businesses, ensuring that every purchase helps sustain the local community and its unique flavors.

Cork and Craft specializes in curating bespoke wine and beer packages perfect for large remote teams, facilitating virtual happy hours that boost team morale and foster a sense of community.

At Cork and Craft, we offer a range of packages to delight every palate, including our 'Classic' package that features a selection of foundational wine and beer flights, and the 'Seasonal' package that contains handpicked offerings designed to embody the character of each season. For those who love to curate their own experience, our 'Build Your Own' option allows the freedom to mix and match from our premier collection of local wines and craft beers.

Cork and Craft is dedicated to sustainability, sourcing our selection of wines and beers from local Connecticut producers to minimize transportation emissions and support the local economy. We also prioritize eco-friendly packaging and encourage our partners to adopt sustainable practices, ensuring that our love for fine beverages goes hand in hand with respect for the environment.

Each Cork and Craft tasting package comes with an educational twist; our included Learning Cards detail the origin, ideal serving temperature, AV, and potential pairings for each beverage. Additionally, Flavor Profiles are provided to enrich the tasting experience, ensuring you fully appreciate the nuances of each sip, making our packages perfect for both enjoyment and learning. Furthermore, an example of a team building activity with instructions is provided.

To order a tasting package for your team, simply select your preferred assortment of artisanal wines and craft beers, customize the contents to your team's tastes, and click the 'Add to Cart' option on our website. After checkout, we'll handle the rest, ensuring a curated tasting experience is delivered straight to your team's doorsteps, ready for your next virtual gathering.
